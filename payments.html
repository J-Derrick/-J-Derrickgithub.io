About Q-Payments


Leverage our communication solutions to meet your business needs

Introducing a cutting-edge payment gateway solution designed specifically for businesses in Kenya, leveraging the popular M-Pesa mobile wallet to streamline transactions and enhance customer experience. This innovative solution integrates M-Pesa's STK Push notifications, providing a seamless and secure payment method for both online and in-store transactions.

M-Pesa Integration

At the heart of our solution is the integration of M-Pesa, the most widely used mobile money transfer service in Kenya. By integrating M-Pesa, we ensure that our customers can make payments using their mobile wallets, significantly enhancing the convenience and accessibility of our payment gateway. This integration is achieved through the Daraja API, offering a robust and secure platform for transactions.


STK Push Notifications

Our solution also incorporates M-Pesa's STK Push notifications, a feature that sends payment requests directly to customers' mobile phones. This method eliminates the need for customers to manually enter payment details, streamlining the checkout process and improving the overall customer experience. With STK Push, customers can simply confirm the payment request on their phones, making transactions faster and more efficient.
